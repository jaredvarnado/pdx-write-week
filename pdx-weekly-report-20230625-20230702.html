PDX Write Week 06/25/2023-07/02/2023
Welcome to PDX Write Week, a roundup of literary events including readings, workshops, social mixers, and classes for the Portland, OR area. This newsletter covers events Thursday through Wednesday of the upcoming week from 06/25/2023 through 07/02/2023.

If there's an upcoming event that PDX Write Week should know about please email the details pdxwriteweek@gmail.com! The final list of events for the upcoming week (Thursday through Wednesday) is compiled the Tuesday before sending and every effort will be made to include events reported before that deadline.

If you find value in PDX Write Week, tell folks about it and consider following pdxwriteweek on Twitter where updates and conversation will continue all week long. Here is a link you can share for signups (now in a format that won't make you repeat random characters!): http://bit.ly/pdxwriteweek

PDX Write Week stands in solidarity with the WGA in the ongoing strike. Support all writers! #DoTheWriteThing
